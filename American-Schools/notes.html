  • Numbers:Students per High school = 10/400 - 1000/4000 High Schools = 23'000

3. School Safety in the U.S.

  • Challenges: School shootings, bullying, cyberbullying.
  • Security Measures: Metal detectors, on-site security officers, emergency drills, surveillance cameras.
  • Preventive Programs: Anti-bullying campaigns, mental health resources.

4. Extracurricular Activities

  • Sports Teams: Football, basketball, baseball, etc.
  • Clubs: Debate, music, drama, robotics.
  • Community Involvement: Volunteer programs and charity events.

5. Fun Facts

  • Prom Night: A formal dance event for high school students.
  • Graduation Ceremony: Students wear caps and gowns.
  • School Spirit: Mascots, pep rallies, team chants.

6. Vocabulary

  • Grades: Marks given for schoolwork (A, B, C, D, F).
  • GPA (Grade Point Average): Measures academic performance.
  • Homeroom: A class where administrative tasks are handled.
