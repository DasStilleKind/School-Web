Different Types of American Schools

American schools come in many forms. From elementary schools to high schools, each type has its own unique structure and system. Let's explore the different types of schools in the USA:

  • Elementary Schools: These schools usually serve children from kindergarten to fifth grade (ages 5–10).
  • Middle Schools: Middle schools serve students between the ages of 11–13, typically grades 6–8.
  • High Schools: High schools cater to students in grades 9–12, ages 14–18, and prepare them for college or vocational training.
